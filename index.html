How to Use
  1. Select a genre from the drop-down menu above. Game cards will populate the grid to the right.
  2. Click the last card in the grid to add more cards and find more upcoming games.
  3. Click on a game card to show more information about that game in a popup window. Press the "X" at the top right of the window or click outside of it to close it.
  4. Click the "scroll to top" button at the bottom-right of the screen to return to the top of the grid.
